## What Stage Is

Stage is a proprietary AI search positioning methodology developed by Cast Iron LA. Core insight: AI systems function as probability-collapse engines, not retrieval systems. Brands must optimize for selection, not visibility.

**Current positioning:**
- **Your website is where trust converts. Not where it starts.** (SGO tagline)
- **Stage doesn't match the keyword. It matches the human who types it.** (Stage tagline)

**Latest breakthrough (March 2026):**  
The **Negative Anchor effect** — AI systems weight criticism-with-resolution higher than praise alone. Resolution signals integrity. This led to a new product concept: **Resolution Architecture** (or Integrity Architecture) — brands publicly documenting their own criticism, response, and change as a structured AI synthesis signal.
